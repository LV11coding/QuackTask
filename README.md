# 🎯 QuackQuest: The Gamified Mentorship Hub

**QuackQuest** is a web-based ecosystem designed to transition mentorship from chaotic instant messaging into a structured, high-engagement environment. By treating learning as a "Quest," it replaces the noise of Viber with organized progress and healthy competition.

---

## 🚀 Core Functionalities

### 📜 Mission Board (Task Management)
The Mentor issues "Missions" (tasks) through a dedicated interface rather than a scrolling chat. 
* **Metadata:** Every mission is tagged with a **Difficulty Level** and a **Deadline**.
* **Rewards:** Completing a mission grants a specific amount of **XP (Experience Points)**.

### 🏆 Hall of Fame (Leaderboard)
To drive engagement through gamification, the platform tracks student progress in real-time.
* **User Profiles:** Every student has a personalized profile showcasing their achievements.
* **Noble Competition:** Points accumulated from missions determine rankings, encouraging students to stay active and consistent.

### 📚 The Library (Knowledge Base)
In standard chat apps, important resources are buried under hundreds of messages. QuackQuest provides a permanent home for:
* **Categorized Resources:** Links, tutorials, and documentation organized by topic.
* **Persistence:** Critical information is always searchable and never "lost" in history.

### 💬 Contextual Q&A (Threaded Discussions)
To eliminate the confusion of a single "General Chat," the platform uses a threaded system:
* **Mission-Specific Comments:** Every mission has its own dedicated discussion area.
* **Clarity:** Questions are asked and answered directly where the task is located, keeping the technical context intact.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Requirement: Live Connectivity
The platform is designed to maintain a constant **online connection** between all users, enabling real-time updates for leaderboard shifts, new mission alerts, and instant feedback between mentors and students.
